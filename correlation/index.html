Correlation Disclaimer

Correlations shown by the app do not establish causation and should not be interpreted as scientific or medical conclusions.

Patterns displayed in charts, reports, and analytics — including comparisons between days with and without a given herb or supplement — are based on your own logged data. They are intended for personal self-observation and exploration only.

A correlation between two variables does not mean one caused the other. Many factors can influence how you feel, including sleep, stress, diet, exercise, medications, and conditions not captured in the app.

Do not make health, treatment, or lifestyle decisions based solely on in-app correlations. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical guidance.
