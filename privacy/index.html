Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this application (“App”) collects, uses, and protects information provided by users.

Information We Collect

The App may collect information you choose to enter, including:

  • Account information (such as email address)
  • Herb, plant, fungi, supplement, nootropic, nutrition, symptom, wellness, and self-observation data
  • Mood, energy, focus, stress, anxiety, sleep, symptom, biometric, and wellness observations
  • Notes, journal entries, and personal observations
  • Device and application usage information
  • Optional health and fitness information authorized through Apple Health

How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • Provide App functionality
  • Synchronize data across devices
  • Generate reports, charts, trends, and correlations
  • Generate AI-powered summaries, observations, and wellness insights
  • Improve application performance and features
  • Respond to support requests and user feedback

Apple Health Data

If you choose to connect Apple Health, the App may access health and fitness information that you explicitly authorize. Examples may include:

  • Heart rate
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Sleep metrics
  • Activity data
  • Workouts
  • Other Apple Health categories you choose to share

Health data is used solely to provide App functionality, including tracking, charts, trend analysis, correlations, and user-requested insights. Health data is not sold to advertisers or data brokers. You may revoke Apple Health permissions at any time through your device settings. The App only accesses the categories of Apple Health data that you explicitly authorize.

See also: Apple Health Data.

Artificial Intelligence Features

The App may use artificial intelligence (AI) to generate summaries, observations, correlations, educational content, reports, and wellness insights based on user-provided information. AI-generated content may be inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, or inappropriate for individual circumstances. AI-generated content is provided for informational and educational purposes only and should not be considered medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or professional healthcare guidance.

Data Ownership

You retain ownership of the data you enter into the App.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect user information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Third-Party Services

The App may utilize third-party providers for authentication, cloud storage, analytics, subscription management, payment processing, Apple Health integration, or AI-powered features. These providers may process information as necessary to provide their services.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data, subject to technical and legal requirements.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any revised policy.
